Some points and problems to note: 

-I decided to go all-out with this unit; giving him animations both for a combat and a non-combat unit! I even 
made a "defend" animation!

-Not too satisfied with the fidget animation. I think he moves his feet too much...

-Due to a couple of problems, I had to render the death animation about 3 times! I'm pretty sick of it now! :p

-I don't have any sounds which match up with the "defend" animation :( This is because he essentially 
re-fortifies from the fortify for 10 frames, then has an additional 20-frame animation which is essentially 
"Attack D"!

-The green in the units32 icon won't transfer to a default units32 sheet - and there seems to be no suitable green 
on it. :( Also, I forgot to remove the shadow... although both are barely noticable.

-After I looked at the unit post-rendering, I was fearful that due to the way he fortifies, he might be a little 
off-centre, and thus his attacks wouldn't hit the opponent. Thankfully, although this is true to an extent, it is, 
again, not too noticable in-game.

-He seems to be off-centre in the death animation. This is because he has to use a different frame size (hense I 
had to re-render him). Would anyone care to fix this?

-Overall, I quite enjoyed enjoyed making this unit, my first in a long time!

A Warrior for all purposes V2   4/2001

Continuing the RPG series of characters, here's a male warrior.  

Extract all files in the zip to a folder named Warrior.  Keep everything together in this folder but feel free to put the folder itself anywhere.  Open the PZ3 in Poser and everything should fall into place.  Due to the texture mapping on most items it may take a couple of minutes for everything to load, but it will.
